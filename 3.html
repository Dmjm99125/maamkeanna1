Ant

Langgam

A very small insect that lives in group(colony) and is known for being very strong for its size and for working hard.

Isang maliit na insekto na nakatira nang sama-sama(kolonya) at kilala sa pagiging napakalakas para sa laki nito at sa pagiging masipag.

Grasshopper

Tipaklong

An insect with long legs that it uses for jumping high and far.

Isang insekto na may mahabang paa na ginagamit niya sa pagtalon nang mataas at malayo.

lazy

Tamad

Not wanting to work or use energy;wanting to rest or do nothing.

Ayaw magtrabaho o gumalaw; gusto lang magpahinga o walang ginagawa.

Hardworking

Masipag

Putting a lot of effort and time into a job or task; being diligent and not giving up easily.

Naglalan ng maraming oras at pagsisikap sa isang trabaho o gawain; masigasig at hindi madaling sumuko.

Summer

Tag-init

The warmest season of the year.It comes after spring and before autumn.

Pinaka mainit na panahon ng taon. Ito ay sumusunod sa tagsibol at nauuna sa taglagas.

Rainy

Tag-ulan

A lot of rain is falling; it is the kind of weather where you can use an umbrella.

Maraming ulan ang bumabagsak; iyon ang uri ng panahon kung saan kailangan mong gumamit ng payong.

Grain

Butil

A small, hard seed of a plant like rice, corn, or wheat.

Maliit at matigas na buto ng halaman tulad ng bigas, mais, o trigo.

Harvest

Ani

The time when crops(like rice, corn, or vegetables) are gathered from the fields because they are already ripe and ready to be eaten or sold.

Panahon kung kailan inaani o kinokolekta ang mga pananim (tulad ng palay, mais, o gulay) mula sa bukid dahil hinog na ito at handa nang kainin o ipagbili.

Hungry

Gutom

Means you want or need to eat because your stomach is empty.

Nangangahulugang gusto o kailangan mong kumain dahil walang laman ang iyong tiyan.

Lesson

Aral

A period of time when you learn something new, usually with a teacher.

Ang aral ay isang oras o panahon kung saan may natututunan kang bago, karaniwang may kasamang guro.
